Call for Sponsors - VolksFest 2014

Published: Sun 09 March 2014
By MAVWC

In Shows.

VOLKSFEST 2014 SPONSOR OPPORTUNITY

The MIlwaukee Area Volkswagen Club is looking for individuals and businesses to sponsor trophy packages. Your business’ name or organization will be on the 1st, 2nd and 3rd place trophy in one class. This will be our 21st annual show on June 7th 2014. Proceeds to benefit Children's Hospital of Milwaukee and the Cancer Foundation.

Added bonus with package: Acknowledged via public address announcements, periodically throughout the Show. If you have a web site for your business; a link can be added on our club facebook site. Business flyers or business cards can be inserted into the 150 give-away bags.

Package price is $50.00 due May 1st. On behalf of the club, we would like to THANK YOU in advance for helping to ensure in making Volksfest 2014 a success.

20 CLASSES: • Daily Driver - All Years • Beetle Stock - 67 & Earlier • Beetle Stock - 68 & Later • Beetle Modified Standard & Super - All Years • Beetle Convertible Standard & Super - All Years • Super Beetle - All Years • Bus - 67 & Earlier • Bus - 68 & Later • Type 3 & 4 - All Years • Thing - All Years • Karmann Ghia - All Years • Dune Buggy - All Years • Sand Rail - All Years • Baja - All Years • Special Interest - Trike, Kit Cars - All Years • Water Cooled - Stock 74-92 • Water Cooled - Stock 93 & Later • Water Cooled - Modified 74-92 • Water Cooled - Modified 93 & Later • New Beetle - Stock & Modified - All Years. Classes are subject to change depending on sponsorship commitment.

Send Check and Info to MAVWC 2985 S. Clement Ave, Bay View, WI 53207 or call J Harvey at 414-483-9171 for details.

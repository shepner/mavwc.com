VOLKSFEST 2015

MAVWC VOLKSFEST 2015 will be our 22nd year!!
The Milwaukee Area Volkswagen Club is a not for profit organization of Volkswagen enthusiasts. VOLKSFEST, our annual show, which will be on June 6th, 2015 is a one-day car show with the support of Concours Motors 1400 W Silver Spring Drive, Milwaukee, WI 53209. We have over 18 classes with 1st, 2nd and 3rd place trophies. Admission is free for spectators while registration for exhibitors is $10.

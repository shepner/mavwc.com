MAVWC Cars and Coffee

MAVWC Cars and Coffee will be at Krispy Kreme on 2900 108th St West Allis WI This show will be open to ALL types cars bikes and clubs. After a long winter, this is a great way to start off the car show season. There is a $10 entry fee. It is free to spectators and open to the public

More information: https://www.facebook.com/MAVWC https://www.facebook.com/events/1009295372426207/

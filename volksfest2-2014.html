MAVWC VOLKSFEST 2014 will be our 21st year!! The Milwaukee Area Volkswagen Club is a not for profit organization of Volkswagen enthusiasts. We have over 18 classes with 1st, 2nd and 3rd place trophies. Admission is free for spectators while registration for exhibitors is $10.
